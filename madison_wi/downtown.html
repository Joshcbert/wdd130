Downtown

So much to do all jam packed within a few blocks!

State Capitol Building

Visit the iconic capitol builing. Go out on the observation deck or see if you can find all 6 fossils in the masonry used inside the buiding. Also near by enjoy walking down the pedestrian only state street full of unique local shops

Olbrich Gardens

Broken up into zone you can see a diverse spread of beautiful flowers and other plants. And don’t forget to stop by the hidden Royal Thai Pavillion!

Farmer’s Market

Through the summer and fall Madison has the largest producer only farmer’s market every saturday around the capitol building. Come enjoy some great fresh produce or a sweet treat from one of the many local vendors . Yum!

Children’s Museum

Take the kids to the children’s museum and spark their sense of wonder. With 3D puzzles, an art studio and lots things to climb on or interact with you won’t be able to knock the smile off your kid’s face!

Henry Villas Zoo

Visit Henry Vilas Zoo! This zoo is free to come and visit as part of the conditions set when the Vilas donated the land to the city of Madison in honor of their son who died at 27 years old to to health complications.

Monona Trail

Enjoy the beautiful Monona trail. This paved path circles around Lake Monona. It is great for biking, walking or running. Stop at one of the many parks off the trail and play some volleyball too. Or watch the sail boats out on the water. A perfect place to get a picturesque view.
